Financial Formula

Return on Invested Capital (ROIC):

\(\mathrm{ROIC} = \dfrac{\mathrm{NOPAT}}{\mathrm{Invested\;Capital}}\)

where NOPAT = Net Operating Profit After Tax

Point out that ROIC is useful to evaluate capital efficiency relative to peers.

Query: Top Product Revenue (SQL)

-- Top 3 products by revenue for the quarter
SELECT product_name, SUM(revenue) AS total_revenue
FROM sales
WHERE sale_date BETWEEN '2025-07-01' AND '2025-09-30'
GROUP BY product_name
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC
LIMIT 3;

Use this to validate the revenue attribution in the data warehouse.

Recommend running this in the analytics environment and cross-checking with finance ledger.
